  • tailor the content contained within The League of Legends Marksman Hub to better suit our visitor demographic.

  • Cambridge Analytica in order to facilitate data analysis that provides direct feedback relating to the content and operation of The League of Legends Marksman Hub.

All of your personal information is stored securely, with redundancies in place to ensure information is not lost and/or modified.
